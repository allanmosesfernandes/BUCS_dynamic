The Entertainments department are looking to recruit Events Assistants to assist with the operation of their diverse events program, across all three campuses.

Role responsibilities include (but are not limited to) managing ticket scanning, on the door sales and guestlist entry, running the cloakroom for customers, assisting with the promotion of events through flyering and postering, and delivering excellent levels of customer service.

We are looking for confident individuals who are reliable, can solve problems under pressure, and work well with a wide range of people.
