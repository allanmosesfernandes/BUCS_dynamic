What I'm working on:

- Investigate the “Success-for-all” process and further develop procedures to give all students an equal footing.
- Change the NEC policy to have more of a focus on pastoral care to enable students to get back on track as soon as possible.
- Introduce workshops to help academic transitions into university ensuring that students feel like they belong within the NTU community.
- Review Student Code of Behaviour (SCOB) procedures to ensure that university actions are not hindering student performance.
- Restructured academic representation with a focus on student experience.
- Worked with NTU to create a library advisory board enabling students to dictate the services and procedures in the library.
- Updated academic support guidance covering; NECs, Academic Appeals, and Academic Irregularities.
- Created a new Apprentice E&D Network to ensure they receive appropriate support.
- Implemented dedicated working groups to help course reps on different study pathways, including: apprenticeship, long distance and mature.
