Remedying this can be complicated, but in general, tenants should take steps such as:
  • keeping the bathroom door shut during and after a bath or shower
  • keeping large items away from walls
  • avoiding drying clothes in a room with no ventilation
  • closing the kitchen door and opening a window or using an extractor fan when cooking
  • covering pans when boiling
  • maintaining low constant heating and increasing it as and when required
  • ensuring the landlord is aware of any heating problems that could allow damp or condensation to linger
