Sustainability at NTSU

Sustainability is more than just a word. There's a sustainability dimension to everything we do (as individuals and as an organisation).

At its core, sustainability refers to the societal goal for people and nature to safely coexist on earth for as long as possible.

The United Nations have developed a set of 17 Sustainable Development Goals to help guide sustainable practices worldwide. NTSU's 2025 Strategy holds our sustainable progress accountable according to these goals.
