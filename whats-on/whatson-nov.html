Movember: What’s on this week

Men are dying too young–and largely for preventable reasons. That's why NTSU and NTU are raising funds for Movember this month. Here’s what you can get involved in this week:

  • Tuesday 22nd November, 6pm, Meet at City SU – Weekly 5k run and Men’s chat in The Loft.
     

  • Saturday 26th November: District - £1 District entry with a real or fake moustache.
     

  • Sunday 13th November, 6pm, The Loft: Quiz Night - We're hosting a magnificent Movember Quiz
     

  • Daily Fitness Challenge: We're taking on an epic fitness challenge of 200 squats, 100 press ups, 100 sit-ups, and 50 burpees each day.

Islamophobia Awareness Month: What’s on this week

Throughout the month, NTSU and NTU will be running various talks, workshops and events to bring awareness to IAM and to celebrate our Muslim student community

  • Tuesday 22nd Nov, 1pm-2pm, online: Postcolonial approaches to Islamophobia: An Interdisciplinary seminar exploring critical and creative approaches at NTU
  • Wednesday 23rd Nov – Friday 25th Nov, Clifton Campus Library: NTU Libraries' exhibition from islamophobia-awareness.org
  • Wednesday 23rd Nov, 3pm-4pm, online: Is it safe to come out yet? Experiences of conversion among those who convert to Islam
  • Thursday 24th Nov, 5pm – 6:30pm, The Level, City SU: A conversation on… the experience of Muslim students on campus

Have your say to win £100

Do you want to shape your university experience and influence the direction of a leading UK university?

For the first time ever, you – the students – will get to have your say in the Teaching Excellence Framework (TEF) survey. Your feedback will help shape the future of NTU—and that's a big deal.

Don't miss out. Submit your answers and you could win £100

Think you could contribute even more? At the end of the survey, you can register your interest to take part in our focus groups. Alternatively, email Jordan.fane@su.ntu.ac.uk to express your interest. All those selected to take part will be compensated for their time with a £25 voucher.
