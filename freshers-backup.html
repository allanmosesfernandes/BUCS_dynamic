NTSU's Freshers Team is responsible for welcoming new students into the NTU community and ensuring they have the best start to university life.

 

From helping first-years move into their accommodation and find their way around campus to attending our legendary Freshers Week events, team members make sure new students have an unforgettable first week at NTU.
And you can now join the team for September 2023.

 

Why become a Freshers Team member?

Joining the Freshers Team is a chance for you to:

•    Be part of the biggest and best Freshers week in the UK
•    Meet new friends
•    Support thousands of new students
•    Develop skills and experience for your CV
•    Receive a full day of valuable training to prepare you for the role (2 days for Captains)
•    Plus lots of other perks, including a free team top and a Freshers Team social once the week is over. 

98% of the 2022 Freshers Team members would recommend the role to a friend.

Over 90% of the 2022 Freshers Team members rated the experience as very good for enjoyment and a sense of community.

Over 80% of the 2022 Freshers Team rated the events they attended as very good.
